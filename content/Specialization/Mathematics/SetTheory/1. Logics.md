**1.11 EXAMPLE.**
If $E = [0, 1]$, prove that $\sup E = 1$.

<div class="proof-section">

By the definition of interval, 1 is an upper bound of $E$. 
Since $1 \in E$, it follows that $M \ge 1$. 
Thus 1 is the smallest upper bound of $E$.
